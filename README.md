# Preparing Manuscripts for Journals

Workshop materials for a session on preparing and submitting manuscripts to academic journals in the social sciences.

## Authors

- **Charles Crabtree**, Senior Lecturer, School of Social Sciences, Monash University & K-Club Professor, University College, Korea University
- **Megan A. Farrelly**, Professor of Environmental Geography & Associate Dean of Graduate Research, Faculty of Arts, Monash University

## Overview

A 3-hour workshop (14:00–17:00, Thursday 12 March 2026) covering:

1. **Understanding rankings and strategies** — journal metrics, fit, and building a submission plan
2. **Writing for the selected journal** — reading the journal, adapting your manuscript, cover letters
3. **The submission process** — mechanics, peer review, and responding to reviewers
4. **Dealing with rejections** — strategic resubmission, resilience, and the shadow CV

## Requirements

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

## Viewing the Slides

Open `slides.html` in any browser. Navigate with arrow keys. Press `Esc` for slide overview. Clickable timers for exercises.

Slides are self-contained HTML using [Reveal.js](https://revealjs.com/) loaded via CDN. No build step required.
