## Content Outline (~41 slides)
1. **Rankings & Strategies** — journal metrics (IF, SJR, h5, Quartile, CiteScore, FWCI), generalist vs. specialist, target list, submission ladder
2. **Writing for the Journal** — reading the journal, research gaps (Miles 2017), novelty (Sovacool et al. 2018), academic prose types, Uneven U (Hayot 2014), manuscript structure, abstracts (4Cs), cover letters, anonymization, revision, formatting
3. **The Submission Process** — pre-submission checklist, authorship & attribution, collaboration spectrum (Ezard), submission pipeline, decision types, what good reviewers do (Sovacool et al. 2022)
4. **Dealing with Rejections** — normalizing rejection, shadow CV, strategic responses, revision checklist, responding to reviewers, emotional resilience, predatory journals
